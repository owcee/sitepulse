   - Architecture: MobileNetV3-Large
   - Input: 224×224 RGB images

   - For server-side delay prediction
   - Works for ALL 75+ task types

- Training Method: 2-stage transfer learning + MixUp augmentation

- Training Date: November 27, 2025
- Algorithm: Linear Regression
- Features: 7 numeric + 75+ one-hot encoded task types
- Dataset: 300 synthetic samples (realistic simulation)
- Optimization: L2 regularization
